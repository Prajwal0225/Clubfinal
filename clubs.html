Team Ventures

("Powering the Future: Unleashing Innovation in Electric Vehicle R&D")

Introduction:
Our club has experienced remarkable success in recent years, garnering recognition and accolades in various competitions and events. Despite facing challenges such as limited funding and official club authorization, our dedicated members have showcased their passion and commitment to making a difference. This article highlights our notable achievements and ongoing projects, shedding light on our journey as a club.

Past Achievements:
First Prize in the Mobility Section of DIPEX 23:
1. In the esteemed DIPEX 23 event, a Maharashtra and Goa state competition organized by ABVP and SUJAN Trust, our club emerged victorious in the Event. We received the first prize for our outstanding project on "Retrofitment of EV with Regenerative Braking and Smart Helmet." This achievement not only recognized our technical prowess but also demonstrated our commitment to promoting sustainable transportation solutions.

Second Prize for Paper Presentation in Energy Conclave by CMIA:
2. Our club also showcased its research and presentation skills in the Energy Conclave organized by CMIA. We proudly secured the second prize for our compelling paper presentation. This accomplishment underscored our club's dedication to exploring innovative ideas and addressing the challenges faced by the energy sector.

Overcoming Challenges:
The path to success is seldom without obstacles, and our club faced its fair share of challenges. Despite these hurdles, our members remained resilient, determined, and united in their pursuit of excellence.

Struggles for Funding:
1. One of our significant challenges was securing adequate funds for our foundational projects. We encountered difficulties in sourcing financial support, but through sheer determination and resourcefulness, we managed to find alternative means and leverage partnerships to overcome this hurdle. This experience instilled in us a valuable lesson about the importance of resource management and creative problem-solving.

Official Club Authority:
2. While we faced setbacks in obtaining official club authorization within our college, our members did not allow this to dampen their spirits. Instead, we embraced the opportunity to operate as a dedicated group of enthusiasts, fostering a strong sense of camaraderie and motivation. We believe that true success stems from the passion and drive of individuals, regardless of official recognition.

Throughout our journey, Prof. S M Shinde Sir from our college has been an invaluable source of guidance and support. His wisdom and expertise have helped us navigate through the challenges we faced, offering valuable insights and solutions. We are grateful for his mentorship, which has played a pivotal role in our success.

Current Projects and Research:
Our club continues to explore the forefront of technology and innovation, focusing primarily on Electric Vehicle (EV) research and development. Here are some of the exciting projects currently underway:

Hydrogen Fuel Cell Research:
1. We are actively investigating the potential of hydrogen fuel cells as a clean and sustainable energy source for EVs. Through rigorous experimentation and collaboration with industry experts, we aim to contribute to the development of efficient and commercially viable hydrogen fuel cell technology.

Ultracapacitor Integration for EVs:
2. Recognizing the significance of energy storage systems in EVs, our club is engaged in research on ultracapacitors. We are exploring ways to enhance energy efficiency, improve regenerative braking capabilities, and optimize the overall performance of electric vehicles through the integration of ultracapacitor technology.

Club Membership and Recruitment:
Joining our club is an opportunity for students to engage in cutting-edge research, gain hands-on experience, and contribute to sustainable technological advancements. We follow a rigorous recruitment process, which includes registration and interviews, ensuring that we select passionate and committed individuals who share our vision for a greener and smarter future.
Our club's journey is a testament to the power of perseverance and passion. Despite initial struggles, we have achieved significant milestones, showcasing our dedication to innovation and sustainable solutions. As we continue to advance our projects and research, we remain committed to making a positive impact on society and shaping a better future. With the ongoing support of Prof. S M Shinde Sir and the enthusiasm of our club members, we are confident in our ability to overcome future challenges and achieve even greater heights.

Team MUN

The GECA MUN Club was established with the aim: “Engage, Encourage, Empower”, and provides a platform for the youth to showcase their concerns of national and international interests. The MUN asserts the spirit of the United Nations into the minds of young students. This Club aims to develop the skills of negotiation, critical thinking, analytical skills, problem solving skills, diplomacy and empathy among the students of GECA.  The Club is an initiative by the students of our college.

GECA MUN won many awards and also received recognition across many colleges and established itself as a major delegation in the Maharashtra circuit  this year. In the Nashik MUN which was held in the month of June; they’ve won two awards. They have also been a part of online MUN which increased their reach, the club members participated in the KIMUN ( Kuwait International MUN) and one  member received the verbal mention. Following that was the Meghalaya MUN; in which GECA MUN proudly bagged five awards having our Secretary General, Samey Lyngrah, as co-Chairperson for the UNHRC Committee. In The PICT (Pune Institute of computer technology ) MUN conference; the Team won six awards leading them to become the Best Delegation of the conference. The club's members also participated  in the Abhirup Yuvasansad Youth Parliament that was held at MGM College, where the President of the club, Sakshi Sharma received the award of the Best Parliamentarian. In the month of March, the club participated in the VIT ( Vishwakarma Institute of Technology ) MUN where they bagged five awards and also won admiration from many delegates and chairpersons. The club along with the students of GECA participated in the Riverdale MUN with, Samey Lyngrah and Pushkar Bhande were chairpersons for two committees and our students won 3 awards. Overall the year was filled with a lot of learning experiences and achievements for the club and it's members. 

On the special occasion of Constitution Day GECA MUN hosted a debate competition for the students of GECA; This competition received  an overwhelming response from the students of the college. They also conducted a series of podcasts by Miss. Bismah Shaikh. Expanding its diversity, the club also conducted a Digital Poster Making competition for the students on the memorable occasion of International Human Rights Day. 
This year GECA MUN also conducted its first ever ‘Intra GECA MUN ' conference; It was a two day conference organized in the college for our students. There were three committees in conference:
1. UNHRC (United Nations Human Rights Council)
2. AIPPM( All India Political Parties Meet)

The club received an astonishing response from the participants and the conference was a huge success. Talking about the problems faced by the club, the team members smiled and said, ''While working with such a team; you learn to convert your problems into stepping stones ". 

The club was established with the ultimate aim of conducting the inter-college 'GECA MUN Conference '. This year they have moved many blocks ahead from where they had started and now they are planning to achieve greater heights, they believe that this is just the beginning of a long lasting legacy. The club will soon be conducting a recruitment drive to add new members to the GECA MUN family. Check them out on their Social Media Platforms to get further updates about the same.

Team Aryans


All-terrain vehicle (racing car) is designed to be able to travel easily on any road, whether it is a rough road on a mountain plateau or a flat racing track, but with cheap cost, low energy requirements, and excellent performance. For the same purpose Baja SAE competition is organized every year for enthusiastic engineering students. The goal in Baja SAE is to design, build and race off-road vehicles that can withstand the harshest elements of rough terrain. Team Aryans Racing is a team of auto enthusiasts and started their journey to participate in Baja SAE competition twelve years ago and the journey continues to learn with time. Team Aryans, every year, innovate, design a single seater four-wheeled off-road vehicle, and customize industrial-level components in a simplified manner that highlights the best performance of the all-terrain vehicle. Every year we participate accordingly by making past mistakes our strengths and aiming to become champions. Because we have won many competitions before, but we could not achieve AIR1 till now in BAJA SAE India competition. In recent years we have narrowly avoided becoming the BAJA SAE India champion, sometimes with luck and sometimes with time. Finally, our hard work of so many years has come to fruition. We became the winner of BAJA SAE India season 2023. In BAJA SAE INDIA 2023, we won the overall static first place, CAE rank 2, Design rank 2, Cost rank 3, and Best 4WD rank 3 honours at the national level. We are delighted that this victory has brought another honour to GECA College. Every faculty member from the college supports our team's efforts, which always motivates us to perform better and ensures that we always act in a way that will bring honour to the institution.

Team Arambh

Team Arambh, the esteemed astronomy club at GECA (Government Engineering College Aurangabad), has a commendable track record in the realm of space exploration. Their mission is to construct and launch GECA’s own satellite, enabling the collection and preservation of knowledge. With a strong desire to delve into the mysteries of space, the team is dedicated to studying atmospheric attributes and embarking on a self-driven learning initiative.

Satellite Projects: Team Arambh’s impressive endeavors include the proposal of the SHUNYA student satellite mission. Meticulously designed, the project aims to study atmospheric characteristics and expand the realm of space science knowledge. The comprehensive project report was submitted to ISRO (Indian Space Research Organisation) for consideration and potential collaboration.

GECA BALLOON: In another notable feat, Team Arambh executed the GECA BALLOON project. Through this initiative, a remotely operated balloon was employed for area surveillance, capturing valuable data over a span of 2-3 days. This successful projects how cased the team’s resourcefulness in utilizing alternative methods for data collection and analysis.

Events and Workshops: 
Throughout the academic year 2022-2023, Team Arambh organized a series of captivating events and workshops top romote interest in astronomy and space science among students.
‍
1. Star Gazing: In April 2022, Team Arambh hosted an enchanting Star Gazing event exclusively for GECA students. More than 50participants marveled at celestial objects, deepening their understanding of the night sky.

2. Workshop at S.B School: Team Arambh conducted an informative General Astronomy workshop for students in grades 8, 9, and 10 at S.B School. Over 100 students actively participated, gaining insights into various astronomical concepts and phenomena.
‍
3. Guest Lecture-Seminar: In collaboration with CATALYST, Team Arambh organized a guest lecture-seminar featuring renowned speaker Sudhir Fakatkar. His captivating talk, titled “From Beginning till Present,” attracted over 30 students, providing unique insights into the evolution of space science.

Future Endeavors: 
Moving forward, Team Arambh aims to reconstruct their Ground Station Antenna to enhance satellite tracking and communication capabilities. This project will play a vital role in advancing the team’s goals, facilitating further progress, and fostering continuous learning.

कलार्पण परफॉर्मिंग आर्ट्स क्लब जो कलार्पण या नावानेओळखला जातो. “कलार्पण”, अर्थात शासकीय अभियांत्रिकी महाविद्यालयाचा कला उपासक संघ.

खरंतर कलार्पणने पुरूषोत्तम करंडक स्पर्धेत2016 पासून सहभागी व्हायला सुरुवात केली आणि तेव्हापासूनच सर्वांचा एकच ध्यास होतातो म्हणजे मानाचा पुरुषोत्तम करंडक मिळवण्याचा. हा सीनियर्सचा ध्यास त्यांचे तप अविरतसुरूच आहे. आणि याच तपश्चर्येचे फळ म्हणजे यावर्षी आपल्या महाविद्यालयाच्या संघानेमिळवलेला पुरुषोत्तम महाकरंडक.

पुरुषोत्तम करंडक 2022 या नामांकित एकांकिकास्पर्धेत भानगड या एकांकिकेने मानाचा पुरुषोत्तम करंडक मिळवला. मॅट्रिक, विठ्ठल, क्रांती,अशा उत्तमोत्तम कलाकृतींचा इतिहास असलेल्या कलार्पण च्या “भानगड” ने शेवटी इतिहास रचला.

‘भानगड' या एकांकिकेने प्राथमिक फेरीत म्हणजेचजळगाव फेरीत सांघिक द्वितीय, सर्वोत्कृष्ट लेखक आणि अभिनय उत्तेजनार्थ हे पुरस्कार मिळवले. तसेच अंतिम फेरीत मानाच्या पुरुषोत्तम करंडका बरोबरच सर्वोत्कृष्ट दिग्दर्शक अभिनय नैपुण्य- स्त्री  आणि अभिनय उत्तेजणार्थ या पुरस्कारांवरही आपले नाव रेखाटले.   

पुरुषोत्तम करंडक बरोबरच फिरोदिया करंडक यास्पर्धेतही आपण महाविद्यालयातर्फे सहभाग नोंदवत असतो. 2023 साली राम-राम ही एकांकिकाफिरोदिया करंडक या स्पर्धेत सादर करण्यात आली. याही स्पर्धेत स्वरचित संगीत, अभिनय उत्तेजनार्थ आणि विशेष पारितोषिक हे पुरस्कार मिळवले.
